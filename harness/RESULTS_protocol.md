## Evidence

### (1) Parity — protocol preserves predictive utility (no attack)

| feature set | folds | sources/fold | heuristic | fixed_effect (v2) | **geometric median (v3)** |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| full7 | 3 | 2 | 0.632 | 0.632 | **0.631** |
| core6 | 5 | 4 | 0.651 | 0.650 | **0.651** |

The v3 geometric-median combine tracks the v2 fixed-effect aggregator within 0.001 AUC on
real data — the robustness upgrade costs no utility.

### (2) Robustness — one coefficient-poisoning source centre (the causal claim)

| feature set | corruption | linear combine | **geometric median** | gap |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| core6 (5 centres, 4 sources) | 25% | 0.526 (collapsed toward chance) | **0.652 (fully preserved)** | **+0.126** |
| full7 (3 centres, 2 sources) | 50% | 0.368 | 0.368 | 0.000 |

**Headline:** at 25% corruption (core6), the geometric median is unaffected by a poisoned
centre (0.652 ≈ 0.651 no-attack) while the linear combine collapses to near-chance (0.526).
This is the Byzantine robustness the linear combine provably lacks (P-0029 zero breakdown).

**Honest limit (surfaced by the harness, not hidden):** with the full 7-feature set, only 3
centres have complete cases (transfer_embryo_day is missing in 2 centres, per RESULTS.md R1.3),
so LOCO leaves 2 sources -> poisoning 1 = 50% corruption = AT the geometric median's breakdown
point of 1/2, where NO robust aggregator can help. The robustness claim therefore requires
>= 3 honest sources; the core6 ablation (5 centres) provides them. This is a DATA limitation
(missing feature), not a method failure.

### (3) DP utility curve — geometric median + clipped Gaussian noise (core6)

| noise (sigma~eps) | AUC |
|---|---|
| inf (none) | 0.651 |
| ~5 | 0.648 |
| ~2 | 0.640 |
| ~1 | 0.567 |

Monotone degradation; utility retained at moderate budgets, collapsing only at the tightest.
Noise is applied AFTER L2 clipping (sensitivity = 2·norm_bound); for a formal (ε,δ) the scale
is set by `vcko.privacy.dp.calibrate_sigma`. The σ~eps labels here are utility probes, not
calibrated ε values.

## Caveats (do not overclaim)

- Complete-case selection bias inflates all AUCs uniformly (live-birth 0.417 complete vs 0.228
  overall); affects absolute AUC, NOT the robust-vs-linear comparison.
- No formal joint privacy-utility-robustness rate is claimed (open gaps G16/G11').
- The cryptographic ZK/commitment layer is documented as the production substrate; this
  validates the protocol LOGIC (validity predicate, robust combine, exact masking).
